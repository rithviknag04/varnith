Mathematical Theory

Mathematics is a subject that is taught from school Elementary (SD) up to Higher Education (PT). This shows how important is the role of mathematics in the world of education today this. The importance of the role of mathematics is also seen in its influence to other subjects. For example geography subjects, physics, and chemistry. In geography subjects, concepts mathematics is used for scale or comparison to make maps. Whereas in physics and chemistry mathematical concepts are used to simplify the naming of learned formulas.

LEARNING TARGET

  1. Train how to think and reason in drawing conclusions, for example through research activities, exploration, experiments, show similarities, differences, consistency and inconsistency.
  2. Develop creative activities that involve imagination, intuition, and discovery by developing divergent thinking, originality, taste curious, making predictions and guesses, and experimenting.
  3. Develop problem solving skills.
